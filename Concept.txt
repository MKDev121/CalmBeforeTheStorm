Objective
---------
-Save your farm and farmhouse from different types of storms by building and upgrading your house and farm,
 and strategizing your upgrades.
-Storms come at the end of the week, 1 day after the storm buyers come to your farm house to buy your
 crops.
-The money you earn can be used to upgrade your house and farmhouse ,buy seeds of suitable crops which can
 withstand the upcoming storm.
-At the end of the game if you have big house and farm then player gets to marry their crush. :)
-Cut trees increase your farm and house area.

GamePlay
--------
-Loop: Grow>Prepare>FaceOff>Sell>Grow
-One Day is of 3-5 mins
-In the night player can only stay for 1 min after that they will die and game will be over.
-Tree Cutting and Mining in dungeon
